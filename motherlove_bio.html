Mother Love Bio Con't

She has done over 200 radio interviews to promote Diabetes Awareness since December 2006 with The American Diabetes Association’s I DECIDE to FIGHT Diabetes Campaign. She is a published author, veteran of television sitcoms and radio talk and music shows, film actress, advice columnist and motivational speaker and humorist. An accomplished author, Mother Love has written her third book “Half the MOTHER Twice the LOVE, published by Atria Books (a div. of Simon & Schuster) 2006, penned two advice-based books Forgive or Forget: Never Underestimate the Power of Forgiveness (HarperCollins 1999) and Listen Up Girlfriends (St. Martin's Press 1995). Mother Love was honored with the 2010 History Makers Award from USC Annaberg School of Journalism & Communication, 2009 C.H.A.P. Award for Community Service, She was the recipient of the American Diabetes Association's Volunteer of the Year for 2008, 2007 Living History Makers Award in Arts & Entertainment, the 2006 Women of Courage Award for her work with Diabetes presented by Los Angeles affiliate ABC7. She received the 2005 Eli Lily for Life Award for Excellence in Journalism and the 2005 Urban Leadership International Urban Angel Award for her excellence in community service and philanthropy leadership. She has received many awards, citations and commendations from organizations, local and state officials nationwide.
